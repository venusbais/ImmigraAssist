Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and immigration process

How does the AI-powered document analysis work?

Our AI system uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze your documents for completeness, accuracy, and consistency. It identifies potential issues or missing information based on the specific requirements of your visa or immigration application type, saving you time and reducing the risk of errors.

What types of immigration services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of immigration services including employment visas (H1B, L1, O1, etc.), family immigration, green card applications, citizenship applications, business immigration, and specialized AI-powered solutions to streamline your immigration journey.

How long does the immigration process typically take?

Processing times vary greatly depending on the type of application, your country of origin, and current USCIS workload. Our dashboard provides real-time estimates based on your specific case and current processing trends. We also provide regular updates about any changes in policies or procedures that might affect your timeline.

Do you offer consultation services before I begin my application?

Yes, we offer both AI-assisted preliminary assessments and personalized consultations with our immigration experts. These consultations help determine your eligibility, identify the best path forward, and prepare you for the application process. You can schedule a consultation through our dashboard or by contacting our support team.
